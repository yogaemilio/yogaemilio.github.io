About Me

A few years ago, a close friend noticed my anxiety and
suggested I try meditating. I gave it an honest attempt
during my transition from undergrad to grad school,
but eventually, life got in the way, and I let it go.
Around the same time, my aunt introduced me to yoga.
I instantly connected with it and practiced consistently
for a couple of years until the pandemic disrupted
my routine.

Though I paused, the impact of those early experiences
stayed with me. As the pandemic began to wane,
I found myself finishing grad school and facing
the uncertainty of job hunting—stressful, to say the least!
It was then that I decided to revisit yoga and meditation.
This time, I truly embraced them, integrating both into
my daily life.

Since then, I’ve been practicing yoga and meditation
every day. I pursued further training, completing
a yoga teacher certification, a mindfulness-based
stress reduction course, and attending a 10-day
meditation retreat.

Today, I balance my career as a data scientist at
a university with my passion for yoga and meditation.
I know how challenging it can be to incorporate
these practices into a busy schedule. But I also know
the profound benefits they offer. My goal is to share
this journey with you, helping you discover the benefits
that yoga and meditation have brought me—and
countless others—over the centuries.
